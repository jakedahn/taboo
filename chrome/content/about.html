Saving Your Tabs

Taboo lets you save a tab for use at a later time. After "tabooing" a tab, a screenshot, the current position on the page and information you've typed into form elements are saved.

When you reload that tab you will be returned to the page, at the proper location, with the back button history intact and the form fields filled in.

Using Taboo

To use Taboo, just click on this icon, found next to your address bar. This saves the tab for you.
Saved Taboos display this icon instead. Clicking will open an edit details panel where you can change the title of the taboo and add notes. You can also delete the taboo from here (it will be moved to the trash where you can recover it later).
To view your saved taboos, click on this icon. Clicking the arrow next to it will show the QuickView panel.

In the grid view, you can increase and decrease the size of your Taboos by clicking the "Zoom In" and "Zoom Out" buttons at the bottom of the page.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Action Key Combo
Save Current Tab Accel-Shift-A
Save Current Tab and Close Tab Accel-Shift-C
Unsave Current Tab Accel-Shift-U
Quick View Taboo Panel Accel-Shift-F
