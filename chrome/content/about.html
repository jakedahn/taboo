Saving Your Tabs

Taboo lets you save a tab for use at a later time. After "tabooing" a tab, a screenshot, the current position on the page and information you've typed into form elements are saved.

When you reload that tab you will be returned to the page, at the proper location, with the back button history intact and the form fields filled in.

Using Taboo

To use Taboo, just click on this icon, found next to your address bar. This saves the tab for you.
Saved Taboos display this icon instead. Clicking this again will resave the taboo with current location/history/form fields.
To view your saved taboos, click on this icon.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Action Key Combo
Save Current Tab Accel-Shift-A
Save Current Tab and Close Tab Accel-Shift-C
Unsave Current Tab Accel-Shift-U
Show Taboos Accel-Shift-F

*Accel means "Apple" on Macs and "Control" on Windows/Linux
